Thank you for your donation.

No matter what, how, when, where, who or why you choose to donate to Women's Habitat the positive effects of your donation will help us to help women in fundamental needs. Moreover the spinoff or indirect benefits of your donation will also benefit others.

If you wish for further information about your donation or indeed our organization and our services please use our Contact Page . Please bear in mind though that Women's Habitat may not respond as quickly as we wish. Again, thank you for your consideration and charitableness towards others.
